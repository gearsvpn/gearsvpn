Money-Back Guarantee

Not happy? Get every penny back. If GearsVPN doesn't meet your expectations for any reason, contact us within 30 days of your purchase and we'll refund you in full — no interrogation, no hard feelings.

1. Our Guarantee

We offer a full 30-day money-back guarantee on your first GearsVPN subscription. If you're not satisfied for any reason, contact us within 30 days of your purchase date and we'll refund 100% of your payment. No forms, no justifications, no hassle.

2. Eligibility Conditions

✓ Eligible

  • ✓ First-time purchase only
  • ✓ Request made within 30 calendar days of payment date
  • ✓ Account in good standing (not suspended for Terms violations)

✗ Not Eligible

  • ✗ Renewals and subsequent purchases
  • ✗ Accounts terminated for Acceptable Use violations
  • ✗ Refunds requested after the 30-day window

3. How to Request a Refund

1
Email us at support@gearsvpn.com with the subject line "Refund Request — [your registered email]".
2

3

4. Processing Time

  • Refunds are processed within 2 business days of approval.
  • Funds typically appear on your statement within 5–10 business days depending on your bank or card issuer.
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the refund is initiated.

5. What Happens to Your Account

  • Upon refund approval, your GearsVPN subscription is cancelled immediately.
  • You will lose access to VPN servers and account features.
  • Your account data is retained for 30 days before deletion, in case you choose to resubscribe.

6. Chargebacks

We encourage you to contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank. Chargebacks that are not preceded by a refund request may result in permanent account suspension. We work hard to resolve issues fairly — reaching out directly is always the fastest path to a resolution.

7. Exceptional Circumstances

If you experience a billing error, double charge, or technical failure preventing service use, refunds outside the 30-day window may be considered on a case-by-case basis. Contact support@gearsvpn.com with full details and we'll do our best to make it right.
