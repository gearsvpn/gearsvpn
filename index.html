Questions
Frequently asked
Do you really keep no logs? +
Correct. GearsVPN does not record your traffic, browsing history, DNS queries, or connection timestamps. We designed the service so there is simply nothing to log, hand over, or leak.
How fast is it? +
We run on WireGuard and VLESS REALITY — modern protocols built for low latency and high throughput. With unlimited bandwidth and no throttling, most members see speeds close to their normal connection — fast enough for 4K streaming and gaming.
What can I pay with? +
Card and major cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum, and USDT. Crypto lets you keep your subscription fully pseudonymous. Each plan is a single charge for the term you choose.
What is the difference between the plans? +
Starter covers the essentials on up to 3 devices. Plus adds more devices, REALITY obfuscation, streaming-tuned servers and ad-blocking. VIP unlocks unlimited devices, a dedicated IP, port forwarding and priority support.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Every plan is a rolling subscription you can cancel in one click from your dashboard — no contracts and no retention hoops. Your access continues until the end of the paid term.
Is there a money-back guarantee? +
Every plan is backed by a 30-day money-back guarantee. If GearsVPN is not for you, contact support within 30 days of your purchase for a full refund — no questions asked.
